Frequently Asked Questions

What is background removal?

Background removal is an AI-powered process that identifies the main subject of an image and removes everything else, leaving a transparent background.

Which image formats are supported?

We currently support JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats. All processed images are returned as transparent PNGs.

Is the uploaded image stored?

No, your privacy is important. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after the session ends.

How long does processing take?

Depending on the image complexity and server load, it usually takes between 2 to 5 seconds to process an image using our AI models.
